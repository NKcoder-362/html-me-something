WHAT I LIKE IN ST. LOUIS !

One of my favorite places to visit in St. Louis is the Zoo. I am proud that our city continues to offer this as a free resource. It is difficult to pick which exhibit I enjoy most. I know many people enjoy the penguin house (especially on hot days). I very much enjoy the water animals (seals, penguins, polar bears) but I can't resist interacting with the primates.

While the St. Louis Zoo has the animals, Missouri Botanical Gardens is the place to experience the plant life. I also enjoy taking the small children in my life so they can enjoy the playground and feeding the fish. The Garden also has times when residents of St. Louis City/Co. have free access to the Garden.

Whenever relatives come to town there are three places I always take them, Amighettis and Ted Drews. That being said, St. Louis has a wide variety of places to eat. I remember my Uncle asked me to take him to White Castles when he came to town. Having grown up here, he has fond memories of their burgers. Whether you enjoy Italian, Mexican, German, French, Chinese, Tai, I could go on, you can get it here.
